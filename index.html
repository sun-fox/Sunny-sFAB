Spinx FAB

Get started with spinx FAB, it's really easy!
Step 01

Visit the github link of Spinx FAB, fork it and clone it onto your machine.

Command: git clone "the path of cloned repository on your github profile.(Without inverted commas)

Step 02

Copy the html content of "fab.html" from your copy of Spinx FAB repository and paste it in your index.html page or wherever it's needed.

Step 03

Copy the casscading styleheet content of "style.css" from your copy of Spinx FAB repository and paste it in your "main.css" page or you can just copy the "style.css" file and paste in the same directory where you keep your CSS files.

Step 04

You will have to adjust the font-awesome link as mine may or may not be comaptible with your Font-awesome version.(For reference, i have used Font-Awesome-5),rest is fine and you are good to go.

Yes, I have a Font-Awesome-5 icons there for design, but you may customize the stuff around.
